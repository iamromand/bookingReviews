    The location is excellent - close enough to the centre to be convenient and yet far enough away not to be too busy. There is free internet at the hotel, though an internet cafe is just around the corner, open till the early hours. The river and Charlottenberg "castle" (with its extensive beautiful grounds free and safe to wander in) are perhaps ten minutes' walk away, and there are enough restaurants and cafes nearby to make eating out easy and a pleasure. I particularly recomment the Italian restuarant just opposite the hotel's entrance, and the cafe "Anker" near the Charlottenburg castle bridge. The Underground station Bismarckstrasse on the useful Pankow to Ruhleben line is metres away and has easy links elsewhere - go three stops citywards to the Zoological Gardens station where you can connect with many other routes. The hotel itself is clean and comfortable and the breakfast was truly excellent. All this, along with friendly, efficient and helpful staff made my stay here an extremely pleasant experience, and the Hotel is to be congratulated. I shall certainly aim to return next time I'm in Berlin.
